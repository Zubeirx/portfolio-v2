§ 01

I'm focused on building products people actually use — starting from the real problem, shipping the smallest version that solves it, then learning from how people respond.

Right now I'm learning by shipping. I'm building StretchSmart, a small consumer product, and writing about what I notice along the way — what users actually want, what to cut, and where new tools like AI genuinely help versus just get in the way.

My goal is simple: join a team where I can keep learning from people who are great at this, contribute thoughtfully, and help ship products that respect the people who use them.
